Privacy Policy — Moo Woof Meow

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Moo Woof Meow ("the App") is a simple animal sounds app for toddlers, developed by Rajan Annadurai. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information in connection with your use of the App.

Our commitment is simple: we do not collect, store, or share any data from you or your child.

Information We Collect

We collect no information. The App does not:

  • Collect personally identifiable information (name, email, phone, location)
  • Use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • Require or offer an account, sign-in, or registration
  • Access the camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or location
  • Connect to the internet
  • Store any user-generated content
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for children ages 5 and under and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Because we collect no data from any user — including children — no parental consent mechanism is required. There is no data collection to consent to.

Third-Party Services

The App uses no third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Data Sharing

We have no data to share. No information is transmitted to us or to any third party at any time.

Changes to This Policy

If a future version of the App ever collects data, this Privacy Policy will be updated and revised privacy disclosures will be submitted to the App Store before the change takes effect.
